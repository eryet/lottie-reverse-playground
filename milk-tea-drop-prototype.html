Motion Prototype

Milk tea drop, double bounce, pearl pop, straw wiggle.

A 3 second canvas loop built from explicit motion beats: a falling cup, two compression rebounds, a staggered burst of boba pearls, and a decaying straw wiggle before the loop resets.

Primary tuning constants

Drop and bounce

Adjust DROP_END, BOUNCE_1_END, BOUNCE_2_END, FIRST_BOUNCE_HEIGHT, and SECOND_BOUNCE_HEIGHT to tune impact timing and rebound energy.

Pearl burst

Adjust BASE_PEARLS to change the in-cup boba cluster and DROPLETS for the small tea splash near the lid.

Straw wiggle

Adjust STRAW_WIGGLE_START, STRAW_WIGGLE_END, and STRAW_WIGGLE_DEG to make the straw react earlier, later, or with more elastic snap.
