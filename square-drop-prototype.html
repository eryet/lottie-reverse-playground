Motion Prototype

Square drop with squash, rebound, and settle.

This simple prototype uses three explicit phases: fall, impact rebound, and settle. The motion is intentionally clean and easy to tune so later review passes can focus on timing and feel rather than structure.

Phase: Fall

Primary tuning constants

Fall speed

Adjust DROP_DURATION, DROP_START_Y, and GROUND_Y to control how quickly the square reaches the floor and where contact happens.

Squash amount

Adjust IMPACT_SQUASH_X, IMPACT_SQUASH_Y, BLOCK_DEPTH_X, and BLOCK_DEPTH_Y to control impact deformation and how dimensional the block feels.

Settle timing

Adjust REBOUND_DURATION, REBOUND_HEIGHT, and SETTLE_DURATION to control the rebound size and how long the square takes to come to rest.
